When people talk about Chuck’s Casse-Croute in Gatineau, most think of poutine first.
And they are right.

But what truly sets Chuck’s apart is its unique house gravy.

## A gravy with a real identity

This is not a standard gravy.
It is not industrial or generic.

It is rich, thick, flavorful, and full of character.

Many customers say this is what makes Chuck’s poutine stand out from other snack bars in Gatineau.
The gravy does not just cover the fries. It defines the brand.

## The club sandwich: a classic that earns its spot

People talk about poutine, but regulars know Chuck’s club sandwich is a must-try.

Perfectly toasted bread, generous chicken, crispy bacon, crunchy lettuce, fresh tomatoes.
A true Quebec snack-bar club sandwich: simple, satisfying, and done right.

Pair it with a small poutine and house gravy, and you quickly understand why people come back.

## A local spot rooted in its community

Chuck’s is more than food. It is a local business that gets involved:

- Facebook contests with gift certificates from local small businesses
- Cross-promotion of other local companies
- Presence and support in events like GPW (Gatineau Pro Wrestling)

People do not just go to Chuck’s to eat.
They go because they want to support a business that supports others.
